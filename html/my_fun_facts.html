Favorite Book

2BR02B by Kurt Vonnegut

This science fiction short story is about an utilitarian society in which the cure to aging as been found. However, in order to prevent overpopulation, people must sacrifice their life to allow the new generation to come in. A father and his wife are due to give birth to twins, but since there had yet been a person to die to allow room for the twins, the father must make a decision to either kill himself to allow a twin to live or kill both twins.
